SPACE DEFENDER

Survive the Alien Onslaught

How to Play

Mouse Aim Move your mouse to look around (360 deg)
WASD Movement Use W/A/S/D to strafe and reposition quickly
Shift - Sprint Hold Shift to run using your stamina bar
Left Click Click to fire at enemies
Stay Mobile Don't let enemies reach you
Wave Progression Enemies get faster each wave

Objective: Eliminate as many alien invaders as possible before they reach your position!

Scoring: Earn 100 points per kill plus distance bonuses for long-range shots.

Pro Tip: Click anywhere on the game screen to lock your mouse cursor for proper FPS controls. Press ESC to release cursor.
